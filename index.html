About Us

When it comes to rural india healthcare is a luxary in most of the villages in india. Moreover due to extremely large number of contacts and small population everyone knows everyone and hence discussing about some medical problems and personal issues is not only a difficult task but a shamefull one too.
So we with your anonymity as our utmost priority came up with an idea of remote medical assisstance where all you have to do is click a picture and upload and the doctors linked up through ngo's will look at the images and use their expertise in their field to give the best possible advise on OTHER SYMPTOMS TO LOOK FOR and about the TREATMENT IN CLEARLY IDENTIFIALBLE CASES.

Our Technology

All you need to do is send a photo and volunteer doctors and doctors linked up through NGO's THE BEST IN THEIR FIELD DOCTORS WILL BE AVAILABLE TO YOU RIGHT FROM YOUR PHONE.
Keeping in mind the viewpoint of doctors too, it is not feasible to go through thousands of pictures they may recieve so we use MACHINE LEARNING to seggeragate images so that the doctors recieve IMAGES FROM POTENTIALLY ILL PATIENTS where we use FACIAL RECOGNITION TO RULE OUT IMAGES OF PATIENTS TRYING THIS MODEL FOR FUN OR WHO CLEARLY ARE NOT ILL
